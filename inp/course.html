Introduction to Data Science

Learn the fundamentals of data science, including data analysis, visualization, and machine learning techniques.

Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the field of data science. You will learn how to collect, clean, analyze, and visualize data, as well as how to build and evaluate machine learning models. The course covers a wide range of topics, including statistical analysis, data mining, and predictive modeling. By the end of this course, you will have a solid foundation in data science and be able to apply your knowledge to real-world problems.

Course Schedule

Date Topic Description
Week 1 Introduction to Data Science Overview of data science, tools, and techniques.
Week 2 Data Collection and Cleaning Methods for collecting and cleaning data.
Week 3 Data Analysis and Visualization Techniques for analyzing and visualizing data.
Week 4 Machine Learning Fundamentals Introduction to machine learning algorithms.
Week 5 Model Evaluation and Deployment Evaluating and deploying machine learning models.

Prerequisites

Basic knowledge of programming (Python preferred) and statistics is recommended but not required. The course will provide introductory materials for those who need a refresher.

Instructor

Dr. Jane Smith is a senior data scientist with over 10 years of experience in machine learning and data analytics.

She has taught data science courses at several universities and worked in industry-leading companies applying data science to solve complex problems.
