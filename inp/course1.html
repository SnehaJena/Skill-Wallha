Digital Marketing Fundamentals

Master the core concepts of digital marketing, including SEO, social media, email marketing, and analytics.

Course Overview

This course provides an in-depth introduction to digital marketing strategies and tools. You will learn how to plan, execute, and measure digital campaigns across various channels, including search engines, social media, email, and display advertising. By the end of the course, you will have the skills to create effective marketing plans and analyze their performance.

Course Schedule

Date Topic Description
Week 1 Introduction to Digital Marketing Overview of digital marketing channels and strategies.
Week 2 SEO and Content Marketing Learn search engine optimization and content strategies.
Week 3 Social Media Marketing Develop strategies for Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
Week 4 Email and Display Advertising Create and manage email campaigns and display ads.
Week 5 Analytics and Reporting Measure and optimize your campaigns with analytics tools.

Prerequisites

No prior marketing experience is required. Familiarity with using the internet and social media platforms is helpful but not mandatory.
